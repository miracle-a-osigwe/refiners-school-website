About Refiners Schools

The name REFINERS was couched from Malachi 3:3. Established in 2012, on a strong Christian foundation, we inculcate sound Christian values of Faith, Love, Compassion and Respect. However, we embrace diversity in religion and culture, therefore children from different religious and cultural backgrounds are welcomed, respected, and supported.
At REFINERS, we believe every child is different and we are all unique in our ways and approach. Hence, we adopt a Child-centred” process in our academic, emotional and physical engagement. As our motto states “Perfecting God's Will”, we ensure every child is particularly 'refined' that he or she should become perfectly and thoroughly furnished, prepared for every good work in the immediate community locally and globally.
